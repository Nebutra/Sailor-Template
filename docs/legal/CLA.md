## How to sign

When you open your first pull request, the CLA bot will comment with a
sign link. The fastest way to sign is to reply to that comment with the
exact phrase:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your signature is bound to your GitHub identity and covers all your
future contributions — you only sign once.

See [`contributing-with-cla.md`](./contributing-with-cla.md) for the
full contributor workflow.
