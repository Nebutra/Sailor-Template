# founder-cemetery Capability Map

## Boundary

`founder-cemetery` is an ecosystem-product package. It handles company pause,
archive, postmortem, lesson, and memorial artifacts.

## Owns

- Pause-first closing flow.
- Cause inference over supplied timeline summaries.
- Evidence-backed lesson records.
- Memorial publish artifacts.
- Cooling-off metadata.

## Does Not Own

- Company deletion.
- Legal signing providers.
- Global SEO publishing transport.
- Memorial video rendering.
